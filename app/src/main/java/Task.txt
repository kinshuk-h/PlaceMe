Problem Statement 1

Placement season is just around the corner in Engineering colleges. Every year even
after the guidance from seniors and alumni, students face a lot of problems in
preparation and end up not getting placed till the end. So to help the students, the
Placement Committee of the college has decided to arrange online sessions with the
alumni.

Task:

Create a Web/android/hybrid app that has the following functionalities but are not limited
to these :
1. Set of the frequently asked problems and solutions
2. Alumni Connect Portal
3. Problem Discussion Forum
4. Organize live Session for College Students
5. Opportunity Updates (On-Campus/Off-Campus Placement Drive, Competitive Exams, etc.)
